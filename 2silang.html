Places to Visit in Silang, Cavite

Acienda Designer Outlet

ACIENDA DESIGNER OUTLET, SILANG, CAVITE
(KM 48 Emilio Aguinaldo Highway, Lalaan I, Silang, Cavite)
Acienda Designer Outlet Naghahanap ka ba ng outlet store? Swerte ng mga taga-South! Sobrang relaxing ng area na 'to. May mga international and luxury brands like Adidas, Nike, Guess, etc. Open area siya, friends! May mga kainan din like CBTL, Macao Imperial, 7-Eleven, etc.
Pets are allowed as long as naka-leash
No entrance fee
With parking area (na sobrang spacious).

Eladio's Place

Eladio's Place is located at Balite II Silang, Cavite
2 kms away from Acienda Retail Outlet and walking distance from Singapore School Manila - Green Campus.
Our Amenities:
16 meter lap pool
common area
game room with billiard table and coin operated arcade
5 glamping huts (not airconditioned)
2 shared bathrooms (with shower, sink, toilet, and bidet)
cottage with dirty kitchen
refrigerator
barbeque grill (coal not included) We have an in-house cafe open to guests and non-guests.
We serve coffee, milk tea, fruit tea, rice meals, and snacks.
