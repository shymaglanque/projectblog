Places to Visit in Bacoor, Cavite

Flyxtreme Adventure

Flyxtreme Adventure Philippines is the only one who represents philippine Paragliding in the World Cup. We provide different kinds of flying adventure such as Paragliding Tandem, Intermediate flight, Special Tandem flight, Solo Pilot course and Tandem Skydiving.

A U.S. Army soldier posing in front of a church (St. Michael the Archangel Parish Church) in Bacoor, Cavite circa 1900 Established on January 18, 1752, the Parish of Saint Michael the Archangel (San Miguel) in Bacoor is one of the oldest churches in the province of Cavite.
The oldest church in Bacoor Cavite (circa 1669). But it's claim to fame is that this church hosted Fr. Mariano Gomes of GOMBURZA and that during his tenure the church evolved from a nipa hut to something solid built on adobe materials.
