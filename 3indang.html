Places to Visit in Indang, Cavite

Villa Filomena (Natural Spring Resort)

Villa Filomena Natural Spring Resort Indang, Cavite
HOW TO GO THERE FROM MANILA?
Take Jasper Jean bus from EDSA-Shaw to Dasmarinas Cavite. Tell the bus attendant to drop you off in Robinson Palapala.
Fare: 56 php
Travel time: 1-2 hours
From there, take a jeepney or bus with a sign board going to Indang Cavite.
Tell them to drop you off in Jollibee Indang.
Fare: around 30 php
Travel time: 1-2 hours (pag may pasok or holiday tinalo pa ang traffic sa EDSA)

Turn right and look for the tricycle terminal with the sign Federation of Indang

Go to the left part of the terminal and you will see the tricycle going exactly to Villa Filomena Spring Resort.
Fare: 13 php
Travel time: 10 mins

WHAT TO SEE AND EXPECT:
2 infinity pool
Natural spring water
Accommodation are all made of natural material
Spacious parking area
Large accommodation
Relaxing cool climate
Panoramic view
bali inspired
And if you want to achieve a solo picture on the infinity pool to post on your Instagram account, weekdays are the perfect time to go here para di masyadong crowded.
Perfect to sa mag partner at pamilya.

Villa Colmenar Natural Spring Resort

Villa Colmenar Natural Spring Resort (Tambo M.Kulit Indang,Cavite)
09064244079/ 09186999981/ (046) 683 0230

How to get there:
From PITX, ride a bus bound to Indang (70-90php) then ride a jeep going to Alfonso (12php) at the back of Jollibee, tell the driver to drop you off at Villa Colmenar.
From Cavite, ride a jeep bound to Indang at the back of Robinson Pala-pala then ride a jeepney going to Alfonso (back of Jollibee) and drop off at Villa Colmenar.
