Boracay De Cavite

One of the nicest beaches near Manila

Located: Inside Gregorio Lim Marine Base in Calumpang Ternate Cavite

Cavite de Boracay is definite a must avoid place during summer weekends and holy week holidays making it hard to find a cottage to settle swimming things,food and beach bumming stuff. Secure everything especially valuables as cottages were open and prone to all sorts and types of Pinoy looting sessions. Accommodation: Fishing and bonfire building is prohibited for those who opt in for overnight. You can pitch a tent if you like and can rent small rooms for more security and comfort of call of nature facility. You can bring your own food or grill within the beach shore if you have your own grilling stove or "ihawan".
